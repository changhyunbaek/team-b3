To Start, Not All Autonomous Vehicles Created Equal

The Department of Transportation created the first set of guidelines around autonomous vehicles. Their first task: figure out what autonomous even means. Below are their 5 levels.


  • Level 0: No Driver Assistance
  • Level 1: All of the driving is done by the person, but the car can automatically assist with something (think power-steering).
  • Level 2 (Most cars are here): Some function of driving can sometimes shift to the driver (think cruise control)
  • Level 3 (Getting to the action): Drivers are still necessary for more extreme sitations but the car is assumed to be functionally autonomous.
  • Level 4 (Woah, now we're talkin'): This is "fully autonomous" for normal situations. Don't ask this car to merge onto the freeway or drive through the snow for you though... As a side note, my money is on John Deere to get here first.
  • Level 5: For the tech and auto world, the holy grail. This means that your vehicle is just as good as a person in every imaginable scenario
